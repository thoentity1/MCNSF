Company Overview

Beauty Academy International was founded as a training centre in 2002 by Joy Duffield and has grown into a well-respected international training academy. Beauty Academy International has become an extensive beauty product distribution centre supplying hair and beauty products, equipment, and consumables. We will continue to strive to be the market leader in our space through producing highly qualified, quality graduates and stocking only the top premium brands in our stores.

Training Academy

At Beauty Academy International we pride ourselves in having one of the most successful beauty schools in South Africa. At Beauty Academy International we place our Emphasis on individual attention by offering training in small classes. We prepare our students for the world of beauty with the required knowledge, skills, practical experience, and confidence to ensure their success. The Students are practically assessed in our comfortable and relaxed environment throughout the year to ensure consistent standards.

Beauty Product Distribution

In 2006 we opened the Distribution which has grown into the one of the biggest salon suppliers in South Africa. We pride ourselves in the quality of all our products and service we offer. We believe in building personal relationships with each salon / therapist / hairdresser / nail technicians we partner with to assist in building their businesses.

We have built up such a good reputation in the market that we have diversified into a distribution channel for the top international beauty and cosmetic brands through our branches located in East London and Port Elizabeth, and through our online store for our national clients. We have partnered with the top international companies to bring the best brands for your Salon to South Africa.
